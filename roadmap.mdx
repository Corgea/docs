## Overview
Corgea is dedicated to delivering top-notch code fixes, exemplifying our relentless pursuit of quality. Our team is continuously engaged in enhancing our capabilities, ensuring that we stay at the forefront of technology advancements.

Our approach is centered around constant evolution and improvement. By expanding our language support and integrating advanced scanners, we aim to provide comprehensive solutions that meet the dynamic needs of our users.

## Roadmap
We're contiously iterating on Corgea to ensure that we are able to deliver on fixes across languages, scanners and code repositories. 
Our roadmap is a working document that will be driven by customer feedback, and development efforts.

- Fix regeneration to issue new fixes based on feedback
- Roles and permissions for users 
- Projects view to group vulnerabilities by project
- Reporting pages
